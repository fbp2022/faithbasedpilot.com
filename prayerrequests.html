HEART OF THE FORGE
How This Works

Every request on this wall represents a real man walking through real battles. When you open a request and tap “I’m praying”, the counter will reflect that you’ve committed to pray at least once.

  • • Each brother can mark “I’m praying” one time per request.
  • • When you open another request, the previous one will collapse to keep the wall clean.
  • • Use specific details, but avoid sharing anything that would betray another person’s trust.
