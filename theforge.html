Overview

What The Forge Is

A serious, Scripture-based men's community - not a feel-good hangout. Real growth. Real accountability.

Mission Statement

To cultivate a brotherhood of God-fearing men who sharpen one another, stand on Scripture, walk in biblical masculinity, and grow strong in the Lord through accountability, truth, and brotherly support.

  • Masculine, direct, and Scripture-based - not soft or "churchy," but biblical and serious.
  • No fluff, no clichés. Real growth, real accountability, and a safe, adult-only environment (18+).
  • A place for men to lean on one another - something rare in today's culture.

How We Meet

The group primarily meets through Discord. This website serves as the home base for:

  • New member onboarding and expectations.
  • Access to the Statement of Beliefs and community rules.
  • Resources for spiritual growth and event information.

Gatherings are virtual-first, with optional in-person meetups as schedules allow.

Why The Forge Exists

The Gap It Fills

Real Christian brotherhood is hard to find. Men need a place to be sharpened, challenged, and supported.

The Need

Real Christian brotherhood is rare. Many men have nowhere to:

  • Lean on other men who actually care.
  • Be challenged instead of coddled.
  • Be held accountable in a way that is firm and loving.
  • Grow spiritually in a serious, Scripture-based environment.

The Forge exists because growth happens better in community than alone - and because men need other men they can trust.

Built on Scripture

This community reflects clear biblical truth:

  • Proverbs 27:17 - Iron sharpens iron; men sharpen men.
  • John 14:6 - Jesus is the only way to the Father.
  • Ephesians 4:15 - We speak the truth in love.
  • God desires relationship, not men trying to "be good enough."

Everything we do flows from Scripture, not opinion.

Beliefs

Statement of Beliefs

The Forge is built on the authority of Scripture. These beliefs shape everything we do.

The Forge is built on the absolute authority of Scripture. Everything we believe, teach, and practice is rooted in the Word of God - not culture, tradition, or personal opinion.

1. The Bible

The Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God (2 Timothy 3:16). It is the final standard for all faith and conduct.

2. God

There is one God who eternally exists in three Persons - Father, Son, and Holy Spirit (Matthew 28:19).

3. Jesus Christ

Jesus is the eternal Son of God - fully God and fully man. He lived a sinless life, died for our sins, rose from the dead, and is the only way to the Father (John 14:6).

4. Salvation

Salvation comes only through Jesus Christ - by grace through faith, not by works (Ephesians 2:8-9). It is the gift of God, not something we can earn.

5. The Holy Spirit

The Holy Spirit indwells every believer, empowering us to grow in holiness, truth, obedience, and boldness (Romans 8:11).

6. The Church

All believers form the Body of Christ - a family called to discipleship, unity, accountability, and love (John 13:35).

7. Biblical Masculinity

God designed men to lead with humility, protect with courage, provide with integrity, and serve with love (1 Corinthians 16:13-14).

8. Accountability

Scripture commands believers to correct, sharpen, and build one another up (Proverbs 27:17; Galatians 6:1). Brotherhood requires honesty and humility.

9. Sin and Repentance

Sin separates us from God - repentance restores us. We bring sin into the light and deal with it biblically (1 John 1:9).

10. Spiritual Growth

Every man is called to grow in Christ continually - in knowledge, character, and daily obedience (2 Peter 3:18).

Community Rules

How We Guard The Forge

These rules protect safety, clarity, and a serious, Scripture-first culture.

Core Rules

  1. Scripture First
    All discussions, decisions, and disagreements are measured against God's Word.
  2. Speak the Truth in Love (Ephesians 4:15)
    Accountability is done with humility, honesty, and grace.
  3. Iron Sharpens Iron (Proverbs 27:17)
    We build - not tear down.
  4. Confidentiality
    What is shared here stays here unless safety is at risk.
  5. No Gossip or Slander
    We protect each other's integrity.
  6. Respect Schedules
    Most gatherings are virtual; participation is voluntary. We respect each other's time and responsibilities.
  7. 18+ Only - For Safety
    Men only, adults only. This protects everyone. No minors for any reason.
  8. Group Chat Courtesy
    Private conversations between 2-3 men should move to direct messages. Open, relevant discussions are strongly encouraged.
  9. Brotherhood
    We pray for each other, support each other, and show up when we can.

Culture & Safety

The goal is a strong, clean, bold environment - masculine, direct, and firmly rooted in Scripture. Not soft, not watered down, and not reckless.

These rules create a safe space where men can be honest, challenged, and built up without worrying about manipulation, gossip, or immaturity.

18+ ONLY: By joining, you affirm you are an adult male. This is non-negotiable for the protection of everyone.
