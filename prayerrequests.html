Heart of The Forge

How this works

Every request on this wall represents a real man walking through real battles. When you open a request and tap "I'm praying", the counter will reflect that you've committed to pray for your brother in Christ.

  • Each brother can mark "I'm praying" one time per request.
  • When you open another request, the previous one will collapse to keep the wall clean.
  • You may use specific details if you'd like, but avoid sharing anything that would betray another person's trust.
